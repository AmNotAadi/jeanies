‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete an outfit?

Regular outfits take 7-10 days, festive wear takes 10-15 days, and bridal wear takes 20-30 days. Hand embroidery adds 5-10 extra days.

Do you provide fabric or should I bring my own?

We have a curated collection of premium fabrics, but you're welcome to bring your own fabric for custom designs.

Do you offer alteration services?

Yes, we provide alteration services for outfits purchased from us, and we can also alter outfits from other places.

Can I see the progress of my outfit?

Absolutely! We encourage multiple fittings to ensure perfect fit and you can see the progress at each stage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card payments, and digital payments. We also offer flexible payment plans for bridal wear.

Do you deliver outside Bhopal?

We primarily serve Bhopal, but we can arrange delivery to nearby cities for an additional charge.
