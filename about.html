Our Story

Founded by the passionate duo Pankaj & Tripti, Jeanie's Boutique has been a beloved name in Bhopal's fashion landscape for over two decades. What began as a small tailoring studio has blossomed into a boutique known for craftsmanship, trust, and timeless design.

Every outfit created at Jeanie's carries with it the warmth of tradition, the precision of skilled artistry, and the joy of dressing beautifully.

From our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted destination for women's fashion, our journey has been marked by dedication to quality, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of what makes women feel confident and beautiful.
